FizzBuzz on the TI-83
    As previously mentioned on my blog post, TI-83 units don't have a built-in modulus function, which forces one to make their own. I did! to get modulus X%Y, I did as follows:
R=Y((X/Y)-int(X/Y))
Anyways, here's the code! Free for you to use :) Just do me a favour and send any friends you impress with it to my site!
:For(I,1,25)
:((I/3)-int(I/3))*3→T
:((I/5)-int(I/5))*5→F
:If ((T=0) and (F=0))
:Then
:Disp "FIZZBUZZ"
:Else
:If (T=0)
:Then
:Disp "FIZZ"
:Else
:If (F=0)
:Then
:Disp "BUZZ"
:Else
:Disp I
:End
:End
:End
:DelVar T
:DelVar F
:End
